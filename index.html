Representation Matters

The video game industry is unbalanced. On average, people that make games and those that play them are completely different. Alongside organizations like BIG (Black in Gaming) and Black Girl Gamers, Padlockes Technologies wants to help diversify the game developer industry. Underrepresented groups (such as Afro American, Latinx American, and women) will learn that they, too, can make games. PadlockesTech must lead by example, currently developed and pushed one game and more will come.

Our first game is "Lockes The Thief", a hip hop stealth action adventure game. It features a black protagonist, which you don't see often in video games.
