Play a black protagonist in this 3D, cel-shaded Zelda-Like. The first game made by Padlockes Technologies. A huge and ambitious project by one person, made in Unreal Engine 5 using Blueprints. Released on February of 2023, this game was a spiritual love letter to the action adventure RPGs from the N64 era. Fight and Sneak. Unlock the mystery of the unRealistic Diamond and protect The Muse, a hyper powerful Sage. To get the game, click Here for the Steam Page. If you like the game, please leave a review.

An anthropomorphic piece of fruit goes on a quest to avenge his murdered apple pal. The enemy, the Vege-Heads, have taken over The Market and plan to build their own empire of juiceless businesses. Fight the Carrot Crew, Brocolli Bois, and more vegetable enemies, as well as their fruitless allies to bring juice back to the market. Work alongside Ms. Cherry, the buxom bad babe, to get the equipment you need to take on different foes. Watch out for the law, though, and don't get caught up in that sauce. now!
