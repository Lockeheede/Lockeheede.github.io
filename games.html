Play a black protagonist in this 3D, cel-shaded Zelda-Like. The first game made by Padlockes Technologies. A huge and ambitious project by one person, made in Unreal Engine 5 using Blueprints. Released on February of 2023, this game was a spiritual love letter to the action adventure RPGs from the N64 era. Fight and Sneak.
